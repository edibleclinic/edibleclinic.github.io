A little bit about us

As physicians who like to inject a dose of healthy scepticism to the myriad of skincare products that are on the shelves, we found ourselves researching the ingredients of every skincare product we bought before applying it on our skin. We want to share all our safer, simplified skincare routines that we’ve landed on, as well as all the skincare formulations and tips we’ve discovered along the way. Stay tuned.
