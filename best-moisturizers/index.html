Best Moisturizers

The Best Moisturizers for All Skin Types and All Seasons

We’ve done the grunt work for you, and compiled a list of the top moisturizers that we’ve discovered over the years with cleaner ingredient lists, as well as things to be aware of before you try them.

Essences/Serums/Toners

  • Primera Miracle Seed Essence (hydrating)
  • Rovectin Skin Essentials Aqua Activating Serum
  • Sulwhasoo First Care Activating Serum Ex (contains alcohol)
  • IOPE Bio Essence Intensive Conditioning (good SKII dupe)
  • Hera Cell Essence (contains alcohol)
  • Missha Near Skin Simple Therapy Essence Lotion
  • La Roche-Posay Hyalu B5 Serum (not drying despite containing alcohol)

Lightweight Moisturizers

  • Primera Soothing Sensitive Cream. For Primera products, try to use them within 6 months to be safe, since they often don’t contain preservatives.
  • COSRX Advanced Snail 92 All In One Cream. This is the perfect light weight cream for summer.
  • Laneige Cream Skin Refiner / Laneige Cream Skin Refiner Mist (good for damaged/dry skin barrier)
  • Amorepacific The Essential Creme Fluid. Contains a few extracts/oils, but is a good moisturizer despite being so light)
  • Make P:rem Safe Relief Cream - contains a few extracts/oils

Thicker moisturizers

  • Moogoo Full Cream Moisturiser - contains a few extracts/oils
  • J One Hanna Cream
  • Sulwhasoo Hydro-Aid Moisturizing Soothing Cream
  • Sulwhasoo Essential Firming Cream
  • Laneige Cream Skin / Laneige Cream Skin Refiner (good for damaged/dry skin barrier)

We’ll share any new moisturizers we find via our newsletter, so subscribe for monthly updates on safer, cleaner skincare favorites, and share this page with your friends if you like it.

P.S…this is probably obvious, but the information presented doesn’t constitute medical advice - always check with your own doctor!
